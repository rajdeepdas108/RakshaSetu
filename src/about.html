🛡️ About Us – RakshaSetu

Welcome to RakshaSetu – a tech-powered initiative dedicated to protecting lives by eliminating counterfeit medicines.

🎯 Our Vision

To create a trustworthy, transparent pharmaceutical ecosystem by combining modern technology with simple verification tools—so everyone can confidently use safe, genuine medicines.

🚨 The Problem

Over 10% of medicines sold in many regions are counterfeit or substandard. These fake drugs lead to serious health complications, economic damage, and the erosion of public trust.

💡 Our Solution

RakshaSetu is a smart medicine verification system where every genuine product is tagged with a unique QR code. When scanned, users instantly see whether the medicine is safe and verified—all powered by a secure backend and optionally blockchain.

🔧 Key Features

  • Unique QR codes on each product
  • Instant scan & verify for users
  • Secure backend or blockchain storage
  • Alerts for unregistered or fake medicines
  • Simple dashboard for manufacturers & authorities

👥 Who We Help

  • Patients and everyday consumers
  • Retail pharmacies & wholesalers
  • Pharmaceutical manufacturers
  • Healthcare authorities & NGOs
